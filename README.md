# 🎓 After the Confetti  
*A slow‑breathing interactive narrative built in JSX about timelines, uncertainty, and the year after graduation.*

After the Confetti is an immersive story experience where players step into the perspective of one of five characters—each navigating the messy, emotional, and often hilarious transition into adulthood. Designed with a warm amber‑and‑charcoal palette and animated chat sequences, the game captures the feeling of reading slowly on a hard night.  
Created with the help of Claude.

---

## 🌟 Features

### **Choose Your Character**
Experience the story through one of five friends:
- **Maya** — the consultant holding it together (mostly)  
- **Dev** — the tech applicant stuck in interview limbo  
- **Priya** — the grad school hopeful with a surprise twist  
- **Jordan** — the startup founder chasing clarity  
- **Sam** — the undecided one who feels behind  

### **Interactive Story Beats**
Move through moments that feel a little too real:
- Late‑night resume spirals at **2:47 AM**  
- Live group chat scenes with messages appearing one by one  
- The rejection that hits hardest for your character  
- The inevitable **LinkedIn comparison spiral**  
- Priya’s surprise video call that cracks everything open  
- A grounding conversation about timelines, redirection, and not‑knowing  
- The moment they laugh at how dramatic they’ve been  
- A **one‑year‑later epilogue** unique to each character  

---

## 🎨 Design & Aesthetic

- Warm **amber + charcoal** palette  
- Late‑night laptop‑glow atmosphere  
- Animated chat bubbles with realistic timing  
- Character‑specific color identities  
- A pacing style that mirrors slow, reflective reading
